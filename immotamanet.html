Immota Manet
“Immota Manet” is a bookazine that aims to tell about wounded territories, marked by deep trauma. The value is in showing the slow rebirth that is there, despite everything, through the dialogues of those who still live there. The pages act like a director's storyboard: where the dialogues between people are matched with scene images and handwritten reflections of the director himself. This results in a documentary on paper that can give the reader time to enter the narrative made up of words, images, inserts, landscapes, and thoughts.
The paper is coloured to divide the sections it refers to: lentil for the cover, black for the earthquake, grey for technical details and maps and light green for the narrative part of the magazine
