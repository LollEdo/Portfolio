Campo dei fiori Gin
The gin itself is crafted using botanicals foraged from the Campo dei Fiori regional park, including wild herbs, flowers, and mountain juniper. Campo dei Fiori Gin is a tribute to the local territory — both in its flavor and its visual identity.
As a designer, I was responsible for the development of the full visual identity — from the label design to the overall art direction. I also curated the look and feel of the brand’s Instagram and website presence personally shot the product photography, ensuring visual coherence across all brand touchpoints.
