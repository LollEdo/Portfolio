Sample Magazine
A magazine that aims to attempt to bring to light the poetics behind everyday materials, speaking through the poems of the workers who deal with the material themselves every day in the factory. The assumed issue is on the theme of steel, and since China is the main producer it carries several poems in the original language that interact with the image in creative ways.
The last page that makes up the flap of the magazine is made of steel and some of the inside paper is metallic, all to faithfully portray the material the issue is dedicated to.
