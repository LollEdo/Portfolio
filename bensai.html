BenSai
It’s an app designed to entertain elderly people who have experience with plants and want to share their knowledge, not only to pass it on, but also to feel more valued and useful in society. Using AI, their expertise is transformed into helpful advice for younger users who are just beginning to care for plants.
Visit the website, entirely coded from scratch: www.bensai.it
