Referto Magazine
This magazine aims to bring to light hidden stories, told through the voices of those who, by profession or vocation, dedicate their lives to the health of people. In these multifaceted stories, extremely diverse experiences are presented, balancing sacrifices, renunciations, pain and satisfaction in a very personal way. This issue focuses on the rescue phase, the first contact one experiences with the world of healthcare. Within this, various contents are articulated: from reportage images to personal interviews and journalistic articles.
The binding and red color recall the medical file and the world of the emergency room.
